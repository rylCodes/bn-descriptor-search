About BNDescFinder

Welcome to BNDescFinder! This platform is a user-friendly, single-page website designed to help users easily explore and access available Business Name Descriptors.

BNDescFinder was initially developed for DTI-Rizal Negosyo Center Business Counsellors, aims to simplify the search process and provide an intuitive interface for individuals seeking PSIC Descriptors.

Please note that while the BNDescFinder is independently owned and hosted by NCBC Daryl Andres, the data and all downloadable resources provided on this platform are sourced from the Business Name Registration System (BNRS).

The core dataset used within this tool is extracted from the comprehensive PSIC Descriptors file, accessible for download at https://bnrs.dti.gov.ph/resources/download via the BNRS platform.

Thank you for using the BNDescFinder. Explore, discover, and simplify your search for Business Name Descriptors effortlessly!
